McCormick Family Pictures

Built with love for Grandma

This project lets family members email pictures to a shared address so they can appear on Grandma’s TV in a rotating slideshow.

It is a small way to keep her surrounded by family memories, holidays, grandkids, trips, everyday moments, and the people who love her.

Family members can send photos to Grandma’s slideshow

To help keep the slideshow private and family-only, please ask Jesse or another family member for the photo email address.
