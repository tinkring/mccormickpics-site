Good photos to send

Keep it simple, personal, and room-ready.

A quick snapshot is perfect. The goal is not polished photography; it is helping Grandma feel close to the people and places she loves.

Send recent everyday moments

Kids, visits, meals, pets, projects, sports, school, and normal family life all work well.

Old favorites are welcome

Scanned prints, reunion photos, vacation memories, and holiday pictures are great additions.

Choose clear, easy-to-see photos

Photos with good light and recognizable faces tend to look best from across Grandma's room.

Skip anything too private

Only send photos you are comfortable having displayed in Grandma's room.

This page is for family coordination. Photos should be family-friendly and intended for Grandma's slideshow.

The story behind the slideshow

About this project

McCormick Family Pictures was created as a simple way for family to share moments with Grandma. Instead of photos staying scattered across phones, texts, and social media, they can show up right on her TV as a private family slideshow. The goal is to help her feel connected to the people, memories, and everyday moments she loves.

Everyday pictures, old memories, and little life updates all help keep family close.
